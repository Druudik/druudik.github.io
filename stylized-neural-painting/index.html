Stylized Neural Painting

View project on GitHub

This website features a gallery of paintings that were generated by my reimplementation of the Stylized Neural Painting paper. You can explore high-resolution paintings, their style transfers and painting videos that showcase the whole process from blank canvas to the finished piece!

The paintings were created using three brushes:
Watercolor, Oil Paint and “Rectangle brush” (this one kind of challenges your imagination).
